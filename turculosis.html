Tuberculosis (TB) Information

Symptoms

  • Coughing for three weeks or longer
  • Chest pain
  • Weight loss
  • Loss of appetite
  • Fever
  • Night sweats

Prevention

Get vaccinated with the BCG vaccine.
Practice good hygiene (cover mouth when coughing).
Avoid close contact with TB patients.
Ensure good ventilation in living spaces.

Treatment

Antibiotics are used to treat TB.
Complete the full course of treatment (typically 6-9 months).
Regular follow-ups with a healthcare provider are essential.
