Hospital Privacy Policy

Introduction

We are committed to protecting the privacy of our patients and their personal health information. This privacy policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and protection of patient information.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal identification information (e.g., name, address, phone number)
  • Health information (e.g., medical history, treatment records)
  • Payment information (e.g., credit card details)

How We Use Your Information

Your information may be used for:

  • Providing medical care and treatment
  • Billing and payment processing
  • Improving our services and healthcare delivery
  • Compliance with legal and regulatory requirements

Protection of Your Information

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information:

  • Secure servers and data encryption
  • Access controls to restrict unauthorized access
  • Regular staff training on privacy practices

Patient Rights

As a patient, you have the following rights regarding your health information:

  • The right to access your medical records
  • The right to request corrections to your information
  • The right to request restrictions on certain uses of your information
  • The right to receive a copy of this privacy policy
